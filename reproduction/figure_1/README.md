## Description
Schematic illustration of the vertex noise model. Two vertices *a* and *b* have latent (noise-free) positions but are observed at perturbed locations *a'* and *b'* within a maximum observable radius *r*. The true geometric edge weight *k = |a - b|* (strong oracle) is unobserved, while the observable edge weight *e = |a' - b'|* (weak oracle) is computed from noisy positions; vertex displacements are drawn from an independent calibrated tempered power-law (CTPL) distribution with uniformly sampled directions.
